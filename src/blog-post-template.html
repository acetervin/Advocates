Key Legal Considerations

Navigating the legal landscape is the most critical part of any restructuring. In Kenya, this is primarily governed by the Companies Act, 2015, and the Competition Act. Key considerations include:

  • Shareholder Approvals: Ensuring that the necessary majority of shareholders approve the restructuring plan.
  • Creditor Rights: Protecting the rights of creditors during the process.
  • Regulatory Filings: Submitting the required documentation to the Registrar of Companies and the Competition Authority of Kenya.
