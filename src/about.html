Our Story

Two Decades of Legal Excellence

Founded in 2005, Romlaw Advocates LLP has grown from a small practice to one of Kenya's most respected full-service law firms. Our journey has been defined by an unwavering commitment to excellence, a deep understanding of our clients' needs, and a passion for delivering justice.

Today, we serve a diverse clientele ranging from multinational corporations and government agencies to small businesses and individuals. Our team of experienced attorneys brings together expertise across all major practice areas, ensuring comprehensive legal solutions for every challenge.
